Lobster

  • Lobsters are invertebrates
  • Lobsters are ten-legged creatures
  • Lobsters have a great sense of smell touch which help them detect their prey.

Octopus

  • Octopus have three hearts. Two for pumping blood through gills and one for pumping pale blue blood through the rest of the body.
  • Octopus have eight arms
  • Octopus size vary from 0.3 to 4 m.
  • Octopus are omnivores.
